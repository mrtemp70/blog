### **How to Change Image Background**:

1. **Select Image**: Use the **Quick Selection Tool** (W) or **Object Selection Tool** (under Quick Selection Tool in the toolbar). Make sure the layer is **unlocked**.
2. **Duplicate Layer**: Press **Ctrl + J** (no need to inverse the selection).
3. **Remove Background**: Delete or mask the unwanted background layer.
4. **Add New Background**: Insert or paint a new background layer.
