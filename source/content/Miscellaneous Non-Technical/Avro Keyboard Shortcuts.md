## Consonant and Vowel Shortcuts:

- **NG** = ঞ
- **Ng** = ঙ
- **Y** = য়
- **Rh** = ঢ
- **Shift+^** = ঁ
- **qq** = ঁ (for mobile)
- **:** = ঃ

## Special Vowel and Consonant Forms:

- **rri** = ৃ, ঋ

## Special Characters:

- **w** = ক্ব (ব-ফলা)
- **Z** = ্য
- **t``** = য় (up of tab)
- **TH** = য় (for mobile)
- **,,** = ্
- **kkh** = ক্ষ
- **gg** = জ্ঞ

## Word Formation Examples:

- **রেফ** = (vowel) + rr + (consonant)
- **কর্ম** = korrmo
- **উর্দি** = urrdi
- **নির্মল** = nirrmol
